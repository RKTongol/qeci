Welcome to QECI

At QECI, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing and stimulating environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Our passionate team of educators and staff are committed to ensuring that each student receives a well-rounded education that prepares them for success in the ever-evolving world.

Why choose QECI?

Here are some key highlights that make us stand out:


Academic Excellence: Our rigorous academic programs are designed to challenge and inspire students, fostering a love of learning and critical thinking skills. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that covers a wide range of subjects, including science, mathematics, language arts, social studies, and the arts. Our dedicated teachers go the extra mile to support students in reaching their full potential.

State-of-the-Art Facilities: QECI boasts modern, well-equipped classrooms and state-of-the-art facilities that provide an ideal learning environment. From science laboratories to computer labs and libraries, we ensure that students have access to the necessary resources to explore their interests and enhance their educational journey.

Extracurricular Activities: We believe that education extends beyond the classroom. Our school offers a wide array of extracurricular activities, including sports, clubs, and community service opportunities. These activities help students develop teamwork, leadership skills, and a sense of social responsibility.

Community:QECI takes pride in its inclusive and supportive community. We foster a sense of belonging among students, parents, and staff through open communication, collaboration, and a shared commitment to the success and well-being of every individual. We encourage active parent involvement and maintain a strong partnership between home and school.

Technology Integration: Recognizing the importance of technology in today's world, QECI embraces the use of technology as a tool to enhance learning experiences. We provide access to digital resources, interactive learning platforms, and cutting-edge technology to empower our students and prepare them for the digital age.
